Privacy Policy

At Nayuta, we take the privacy and security of our users very seriously in order to maintain the trust and prestige that has characterized Nayuta since it began operations. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use and protect the personal information of our users.

1. Information gathering

1.1 Information you voluntarily provide: We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us when using our bot, such as your Discord user ID, your username and avatar, and any information related to messages you send or receive on Discord's servers.

1.2 Information we collect automatically: We also collect information automatically through our bot, such as your IP address, information about your device, and your activity on Discord.


2. Use of information

2.1 Purposes for which we use the information: We use the information we collect to provide and improve our services, and to communicate with you about any updates or improvements to our bot. We may also use the information for research and analysis purposes.

2.2 Sharing information: We do not share personal information with third parties, except as necessary to comply with applicable laws and regulations.


3. Protection of information

3.1 Security Measures: We implement technical and organizational security measures to protect the personal information we collect, including the use of advanced security protocols and limiting access to personal information to employees who need access to perform their jobs.

3.2 Storage of information: We store personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable laws and regulations.


4. Cookies and similar technologies

4.1 Use of cookies: We use cookies and similar technologies to improve our users' experience on our bot. Cookies allow us to remember your preferences and settings, and can also help us analyze how our bot is used.

4.2 Cookie settings: You can set your browser to refuse all cookies, or to alert you when a cookie is being sent. However, if you disable cookies, some features of our bot may not function properly.


5. Your privacy rights

5.1 Right of Access: You have the right to access and receive a copy of the personal information we hold about you.

5.2 Right of rectification: You have the right to rectify any inaccurate or incomplete personal information we hold about you.

5.3 Right of deletion: You have the right to request that we delete any personal information we hold about you.

5.4 Right to restrict processing: You have the right to restrict the processing of your personal information in certain circumstances./p>

6. Changes to this privacy policy

6.1 Changes: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our privacy practices. If we make significant changes, we will inform you of the changes through our website.
